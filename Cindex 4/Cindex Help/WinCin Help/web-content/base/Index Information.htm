Getting information about an index

Cindex provides several ways to analyze indexes and the entries they contain.

To view and modify the structure of records choose Record Structure… from the Document menu.

To learn about the distribution of records under particular letters of the alphabet or under different headings, and about the numbers of fields and characters they contain, choose Count Records… from the Tools menu.

To obtain basic information about the index document, such as the times of its creation and modification, and the time you have spent working on it, together with information about the number of formatted entries and the pages they occupy, choose Index Statistics… from the Tools menu.

To learn about the properties of record groups, choose Groups… from the Tools menu.
